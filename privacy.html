Privacy Policy for Tube Pub Crawl

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Tube Pub Crawl respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is used, stored, and processed when you use the app.

Information Stored by the App

Tube Pub Crawl stores information related to your use of the app, including:

  • Pub visits
  • Crawl progress
  • Challenge progress
  • Drink logs and statistics
  • App settings and preferences

This information is stored locally on your device.

iCloud and CloudKit Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Tube Pub Crawl may sync your data using Apple's CloudKit service. This allows your data to be available across your personal Apple devices.

Tube Pub Crawl uses the user's private CloudKit database. This data is associated with your Apple ID and is accessible only to you through your Apple account. The developer of Tube Pub Crawl does not access, view, manage, or collect your CloudKit data.

Information stored through iCloud and CloudKit is governed by Apple's privacy practices.

Location Information

Tube Pub Crawl may request access to your device's location in order to:

  • Find nearby Tube stations
  • Discover nearby pubs
  • Provide location-based features and recommendations

Location information is used only to provide app functionality. The developer does not collect, store, or track your location.

Google Places Services

Tube Pub Crawl uses Google Places services to retrieve information about pubs and nearby places.

When you search for pubs or places, search information may be sent from your device to Google in order to provide relevant place results. Tube Pub Crawl does not use Google Places for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Analytics and Tracking

Tube Pub Crawl does not use analytics services.

Tube Pub Crawl does not track users across apps, websites, or services.

Accounts

Tube Pub Crawl does not require user accounts, registration, or login.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties.

Information may be processed by Apple for iCloud and CloudKit syncing, and by Google when Google Places search features are used, solely to provide app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Tube Pub Crawl is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
